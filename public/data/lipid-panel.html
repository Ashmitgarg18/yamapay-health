Understanding Your Lipid Panel Results

Introduction: The Lipid Panel test measures the levels of cholesterol and triglycerides in your blood, providing valuable insights into your cardiovascular health.

Systemic View of Impacts:

Organ/System Potential Impact
Heart High cholesterol increases the risk of atherosclerosis (hardening of the arteries) and cardiovascular disease
Blood Vessels Atherosclerosis can lead to narrowed or blocked arteries
Brain Reduced blood flow from atherosclerosis increases stroke risk

Interpreting Your Results:

Heuristic for Negative Results: Elevated cholesterol levels may indicate an increased risk of heart disease and stroke. It's important to follow up with your healthcare provider for further evaluation and management.

Heuristic for Positive Results: Normal cholesterol levels support a healthy cardiovascular system, reducing the risk of heart disease and stroke.
