Understanding Your Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP) Results

Introduction: The Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP) test measures various substances in your blood, providing a broad assessment of your metabolic health, liver function, and kidney function.

Systemic View of Impacts:

Organ/System Potential Impact
Liver Abnormal results may indicate liver dysfunction or disease
Kidneys Abnormal results may indicate kidney dysfunction or disease
Blood Sugar Measures blood glucose levels, indicating potential diabetes or hypoglycemia

Interpreting Your Results:

Heuristic for Negative Results: Abnormal levels may indicate issues with liver or kidney function, blood sugar imbalances, or electrolyte disturbances. Follow-up with your healthcare provider is critical.

Heuristic for Positive Results: Normal levels indicate healthy liver and kidney function, balanced blood sugar, and overall metabolic health.
